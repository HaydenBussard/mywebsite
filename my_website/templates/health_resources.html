Health Resources

Why This Section Exists

I created this section of my website because I understand firsthand how limited resources can be for certain health issues, particularly Lyme Disease and Bell's Palsy. My goal is to provide a comprehensive collection of information, resources, and personal experiences in one place for those who may be struggling with these conditions.

By sharing my own experiences, I hope to convey that I truly understand the challenges you may be facing. Whether you're looking for information, support, or simply someone who understands, you've come to the right place.

If you need someone to talk to or have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact me. I'm here to help in any way I can.
